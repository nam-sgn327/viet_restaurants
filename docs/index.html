If it don't got a number in it, it's not legit: Naming convetions of Vietnamese restaurants California

Why Ali Wong's advice about how to pick an "authentic" Vietnamese restaurant is a surprisingly decent starting point.


In her breakout special Baby Cobra, released in 2016, comedian Ali Wong recounts her (ex)husband's ill-fated attempt to wow her with an authentic dining experience before they were married.

Data from listed restaurants in California, where Wong hails from show that 8% of Vietnamese restaurants have names containing a number having an auspicious (e.g 68, 88) of significant historical event (1975).

Vietnamese cuisine is the next "it" thing for foodies, courtesy celebrity chefs such as Gordan Ramsay, Anthony Bourdain and political figures like Barack Obama.

Methodology

Data scraped from AO3 and FFnet using BeautifulSoup and Selenium. Number of fics on ffnet rounded to nearest 1k from homepage, getting exact numbers would require scraping and summing each individual page for every fandom. Notebook analysis can be found on github.
